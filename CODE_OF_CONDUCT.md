# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining an open, respectful, and professional research environment for the **TARL-Net** project.

This project is part of a PhD dissertation in the field of **Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence**, and therefore requires a high standard of academic integrity and collaboration.

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors and users are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in communication
- Focus on scientific and technical discussion
- Respect differing viewpoints and research approaches
- Provide constructive feedback
- Prioritize reproducibility and academic honesty

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or disrespectful comments
- Misrepresentation of research results
- Plagiarism or unauthorized use of contributions
- Attempting to manipulate benchmarks or evaluation results
- Non-scientific or irrelevant discussions in issues/PRs

---

## Research Ethics

Since TARL-Net is a research framework:

- All results must be reproducible and transparent
- No artificial inflation of performance metrics is allowed
- Any modification affecting evaluation must be clearly documented
- Proper citation of related work is required

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- External communications related to this project

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary restriction of participation
- Permanent removal from the project collaboration space
